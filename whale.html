Blue Whales

The Blue Whale is the largest animal on Earth. It can reach lengths of up to 100 feet and weigh around 200 tons. Blue whales are filter feeders, eating mostly krill, and they can live up to 90 years.

Sperm Whales

The Sperm Whale is famous for its large head and deep-diving abilities. Sperm whales can dive over 2,000 meters to hunt squid and can hold their breath for up to 90 minutes. They are social animals, living in pods and communicating with clicks and whistles.
